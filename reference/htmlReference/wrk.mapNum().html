wrk.mapNum()

Reference contents

Syntax:

wrk.mapNum(num, oldMin, oldMax, newMin, newMax)

Example:

var randomNumber = wrk.mapNum(Math.random(), 0, 1, 50, 120)

Description:

Map ```num``` from the range of ```oldMin``` to ```oldMax``` to the range of ```newMin``` to ```newMax```.
